How it works
01

Text preprocessing

HTML tags stripped, whitespace normalized, and review truncated to BERT's 512-token limit.

02

BERT tokenization

WordPiece tokenization converts text to subword token IDs with attention masks.

03

Inference

Fine-tuned BERT model (nlptown) outputs logits over 5 star-rating classes.

04

Mixed-sentiment split

For reviews with mixed signals, each sentence is scored separately to give a full picture.
